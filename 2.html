AMUR

AMUR는 세계의 주목받는 최고의 기업이 될 것이다.

AMUR provides analysis information on R&D technologies of global countries, companies, and universities based on data that has been filed through patent and thesis preprocessing systems for the first time in its industry.

AMUR has implemented a service that diagnoses the strengths and weaknesses of R&D technology in global countries, companies, and research institutes by applying a diagnostic model calculated bymachine learning algorithms.
